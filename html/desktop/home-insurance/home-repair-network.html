Home repair network

Another great service offered by Direct Line Home Insurance. We know how frustrating finding a reputable tradesman and then making all the arrangements can be – which is why Direct Line Home Insurance includes a handy service called the Home Repair Network.

Quick and convenient, the network’s not just a database of the honest and reliable but much, much more. With the Home Repair Network, if your house ever needs repairs, Direct Line will take care of everything for you (for claims between the threshold values stated on your policy documents). We’ll deal with your claim quickly, find the right people for the job and then liaise with them directly.

We only ever use vetted builders (the kind that actually turn up when they say they will) and we sort out all the costs directly with them. That way, you don’t have to pay them out of your own pocket and then claim the money back from us. And, because we take your repairs as seriously as you do, any work done on your house will carry a 12 month guarantee.

We don’t want to add to your home insurance problems, we want to help you solve them, which is why with Direct Line there’s less fuss and less hassle. The Home Repair Network: saving you time and giving you peace of mind.
