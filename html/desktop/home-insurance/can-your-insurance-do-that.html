What home means to each of us can be quite different. But we all share one thing in common - the need to cover it. Our Home Insurance provides a flexible way to keep your home and belongings covered the way you choose.

3 easy steps

1. Register your claim with Direct Line Home Claims team on 0845 246 8710. We're here Monday to Friday, 8am - 8pm and Saturdays 9am - 5pm.

2. Our claims team can send you a link by text or email, whichever you prefer. You can upload photos/videos of your items and proof you owned them. These can include receipts, bank statements and packaging. When we get these, our team will look at your claim.

3. Once we've got everything we need, one of our product specialists will call you within 8 hours to talk through your replacement and delivery options.

Things to remember

  1. Mobile phone claims: contact your network provider to ensure your phone is blocked. We can't replace your mobile phone until you've done this and the 8 hours won't apply.
  2. The link does not work on Blackberrys and Windows operated devices.
  3. The 8 hour claim assessment could be affected if we're experiencing extreme weather conditions. If this happens, our claims handlers will deal with our most urgent claims.
