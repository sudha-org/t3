Home insurance in Northern Ireland

Did you know that Direct Line also provides home insurance in Northern Ireland? So if you are resident there, apply for a home insurance quote and see how much you could save.

We want as many people as possible to benefit from our great value home insurance – and that includes customers who live in Northern Ireland. So whether you’re a resident of Belfast, Londonderry or Lisburn, you can apply for a quote for buildings insurance or contents insurance just like our customers living elsewhere in the UK.

You can get this quote online or over the phone. Simply fill in our online form or tell us your choice of cover and the required personal details and we’ll provide an instant premium. Once your cover is purchased you can be rest assured knowing that your home and its contents are covered in case of an emergency such as fire, flood, storm or theft.

To find out what kind of home insurance cover we offer simply check out our summary of home insurance cover online. For more detailed terms and conditions you can view or download our home insurance policy documents, which you’ll also find online.

You’ll see you can invest in cover for both your buildings and your contents. Buildings insurance is essential and, if you have a mortgage, compulsory for your home, providing cover in case of structural damage caused by incidents such as fire, flood, storm and theft.

Contents insurance, although optional, is a highly advised investment in case of loss or damage caused by similar incidents. For those who might consider opting out of this cover, you might be surprised how much all your contents add up to. Input your details into our handy home contents calculator online and you’ll see how vital contents insurance really is.

But our home insurance isn’t just for owners of a main residential property in Northern Ireland. Direct Line also provides buildings insurance and contents insurance for second homes, which could include your holiday home in Northern Ireland (Please call us if you would like a quote for home insurance for a second home). It’s important to note that some terms and conditions may apply particularly when the property is unoccupied.
