Home contents calculator

Our policies automatically provide contents cover up to £50,000. This useful tool will help you calculate the value of your contents. For more details on our cover, see our /home-insurance/policy-documents" >policy documents or our /home-insurance/summary/standard" >summary of cover .

  1. Scroll through the whole page or use the buttons below to go to the appropriate sections.
  2. Enter the replacement value in pounds for the item(s). Each time you have completed a section you need to click CALCULATE to reset the sub-total.
  3. Once you have completed all the necessary sections click CALCULATE TOTAL. If you prefer, you can print off the form in landscape and fill in manually.
