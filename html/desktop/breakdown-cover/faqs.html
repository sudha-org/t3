How much does Direct Line Breakdown Cover cost?

Our prices match your circumstances (e.g. car, where you live, and age) like car insurance. If you have a reliable car that you maintain well, you pay less than someone with an old, unreliable car. Our prices are tailored to the person and their car, just like car insurance.

Do I have breakdown cover if I'm travelling in someone else's car?

We give you the choice to decide if you need this level of cover with our Personal Cover option.

Who should I call if my car breaks down?

Please call us on 0800 590 590.

Do I have breakdown cover if someone else drives my car?

With all our cover levels, your car is covered whoever is driving it.

Do I get a discount on Breakdown cover if I am already a Direct Line customer?

Yes, we’re offering all our motor customers a 25% discount off levels 4 & 5 – please call us for details. We also have a genuine no-claims discount that reduces your premium if you don't call us out.

Do you offer all the breakdown cover levels?

We’ve designed our product to provide choices.

What do I do if I need to cancel my breakdown cover?

Simply give us a call and we’ll cancel the cover for you.

Will I get a refund if I cancel my breakdown cover during the policy year?

Providing you haven’t had a call-out in that period of insurance, you’ll receive a refund calculated on a pro-rata basis less a cancellation charge of £10.60 (inclusive of IPT where applicable).

Will customers who have other Direct Line products get preferential treatment if they break down?

No. All our customers will receive a high quality service. The only priorities will be to those in vulnerable situations.

Will your breakdown cover recover my car after an accident?

Yes, we can arrange to recover you following a road traffic accident, however, you’ll be responsible for the costs involved. You may be able to recover this amount from your motor insurer.

Do you give priority to women on their own?

We would give priority to anyone in a vulnerable position in a breakdown situation. This would include drivers travelling with children in their car, or someone in a hazardous position.

What is the excess on my Breakdown Cover policy?

Our breakdown policies have no excess.

How do I know if my recovery agent is really from Direct Line?

We take personal security extremely seriously. We vet members of our recovery network. Our regional agents will carry an ID card and we're able to advise customers of the name of the regional agent we expect to attend to their incident.

How much does European Breakdown Cover cost?

Our cover is charged by the day, so that you don't end up paying for more than you need, and if you take out our travel insurance at the same time, you can save even more.

What restrictions apply when I’m towing my caravan or trailer?

The trailer/caravan must not exceed 16 years of age and must meet our vehicle dimension criteria. Further details can be found in our policy document.

I’ll be going to France, Germany and Sweden - will I be covered for all those countries?

Select "Europe General" under the destination menu on the ‘Your details’ page within quote and buy to get this type of cover.

With European Breakdown Cover is it okay if someone else drives my car?

Your car is covered whoever is driving it.

Who provides the breakdown cover for Direct Line?

Direct Line’s breakdown cover is provided by Green Flag, a sister company who are part of the Direct Line Group.
