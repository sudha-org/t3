Multi-car discount

With Direct Line's multi-car discount, we help you save money when you and your family have more than one car in the household.

Get a discount for every additional car you bring to us. Subject to a minimum premium. Introductory discount.

If other drivers in your household make a claim, your no claim discount won't be affected.

We insure each car on a separate policy. So you can keep separate renewal dates and you won't need to worry about the cost of policies all renewing at the same time. Also, if other drivers in your household make a claim for their car, your no claim discount won't be affected.

Named Driver
No Claim Discount (NDNCD)

Drivers named on your insurance can earn their own NDNCD for every year they drive claim-free - up to a maximum of five years. So if they take out a policy of their own with us they can use this NDNCD to lower their premium. And if your policy ends with us, they can still use their NDNCD as long as they get a quote from us within 90 days.

If you make a claim, the NDNCD will be reduced. Bear in mind, other insurers might not recognise NDNCD.

Only Driver Discount

If you're the only person on your policy and buy an additional car that will only be driven by you, we'll give you a discount for your new car.

This discount works in the same way as NCD, so if you make a claim, the discount will be reduced. Both policies must remain on a one-driver basis in order to keep this discount.

Company Car
No Claim Discount
(NCD)

If you're aged 25 and over and have driven a company car, you'll be able to save money when you take out an insurance policy for your own car.

What we'll need from you:

  • A letter from your company on headed paper that confirms you gave up a company car in the last 2 years.
  • Confirmation of who the insurer, broker or employer was and if you were the only driver.
  • Proof that the company car was not a pool car if you used it for Social, Domestic and Pleasure (SDP) purposes as well as for business by sharing it with your spouse, partner or civil partner.
  • We'll also end up-to-date proof of claim-free driving, equal to the discount requested. You could get up to five years no claim discount.
