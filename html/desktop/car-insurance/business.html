Business Car Insurance

Car insurance for business use

If you use your car for work, you could still benefit with car insurance from Direct Line.

With Business Use, you can name your business colleague as a driver on your insurance policy if they use the same car when visiting clients. Even if they live at a different address.

A car used for business is not the same as a company car. Company cars should be insured by the company that owns or leases them, as fleet insurance.

To get a quote, add your business colleague or find out more about Business Use, give us a call on 0845 246 8701.
