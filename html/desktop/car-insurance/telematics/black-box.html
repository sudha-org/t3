DrivePlus:
Our black box car insurance

Drivers under 21 can get a 25% upfront discount, while drivers aged 21-25 can get at least a 15% upfront discount. Drivers 26 and over may also receive a discount, but if your premium is under £600 you may pay extra in your first year's premium to receive the DrivePlus Plug-in. The DrivePlus Plug-in is only available for cars manufactured later than 2002.

We use telematics technology – also known as black box technology - to understand how you drive. But wait – that’s not all. With the DrivePlus Plug-in you can also find out what you do – and don’t do – when you’re behind the wheel. This way we can offer you car insurance premiums based on how you drive.

How does it work?

  1. When you take out a car insurance policy with Direct Line, you’ll be asked to opt-in to a DrivePlus Telematics policy and enter the code DRIVEPLUS.

    Using the code, if you’re a young driver under 21 you can get a 25% upfront discount that will be applied to your quote. If you are aged 21-25 you can get at least a 15% upfront discount that will be applied to your quote. Drivers 26 and over may also receive a discount, but if your premium is under £500 you may pay extra in your first year's premium to receive the DrivePlus Plug-in.

    Once you've bought your cover, we'll post your plug-in to you.

  2. Got your DrivePlus Plug-in? Time to fit it to your car. We’ll take you through the process with our step-by-step guide. This is very easy and takes less than a minute – no tools required.
  3. The device sends feedback on how you drive. We’ll use this information to analyse your driving behaviour for things like smooth driving, acceleration, and braking.
  4. If you drive safely and get a high driving score you’ll be rewarded with a discount on renewal. To find out more, visit our DrivePlus Portal.

Good to know...

Inappropriate speed? Fast car?
The DrivePlus Plug-in is an extremely accurate device that helps us put together a good picture of your driving behaviour. We encourage everyone to drive in accordance with the Highway Code but speeding does not form part of your driving score. To find out more about speeding, visit our frequently asked questions.

Crowded car park? New area?
Our DrivePlus Plug-in is the best way to track your car’s exact location from anywhere. This could prove incredibly useful when you’re away from home or just forgot where you parked.

Battery problems? Frequent jump-starts?
Get information about the health of your battery through the DrivePlus Portal. The device will give you an indication when it's time to get your battery checked - saving you any nasty surprises. To find out more, visit our DrivePlus Portal.

To find out more about speeding, the plug-in and how it works, visit our most frequently asked questions and terms and conditions.
