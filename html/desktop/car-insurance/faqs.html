What is your cancellation procedure?

If you'd like to cancel your Direct Line policy please call us on 0845 246 8706.

You may also suspend your policy for a period of time if you meet certain criteria. For example, if your vehicle has been written off following an accident and you haven't yet purchased a new vehicle. Just call us on 0845 246 8706. You'll need to continue your monthly payments if you pay by instalments.

When you buy:

If you cancel within 14 days of cover starting or you receiving your documents (whichever is later) we'll return any premium paid less a charge for the number of days for which cover has been given for your car insurance and give you a full refund* for Breakdown Cover, where selected.

If you cancel after 14 days on cover, we'll return any premium paid less a charge for the number of days for which cover has been given and an administration fee of £47.70 for car insurance and £10.60 for Breakdown Cover, where selected.  All figures quoted are including Insurance Premium Tax where applicable.

*If you've made a claim or one has been made against you during the period of cover, you won't receive a refund.

When you renew:

If you're cancelling your policy within 14 days of the policy starting, we'll return any premium paid less a charge for the number of days for which cover has been given, providing you haven't made a claim.

If you're cancelling after 14 days of renewal, providing you've not made a claim, we'll return any premium paid less:

  • A charge for the number of days for which cover has been given; and
  • An administration fee

Full details of any applicable charges or fees will be discussed with you when you call us.

If you're cancelling due to a change in circumstances, you can amend or change your policy by calling us on 0845 246 8706.

You're unable to cancel your policy if there are any on-going claims against it.

Can I drive any car?

Driving other cars is a standard inclusion under our private car policies and allows the policyholder to drive cars that aren't owned by them or hired to them, providing they have permission of the vehicle owner to use the vehicle. However, there are restrictions to this benefit such as age (must be over 25 at policy inception / renewal date) and occupation.

To find out if you have Driving Other Cars cover, check your certificate of insurance.

The cover provides protection against legal liability for death or bodily injury to a third party and damage to third party property. The cover is limited to third party only and should be viewed as emergency cover.

Are my spouse and I covered for business use?

Business cover will be included automatically (when your policy is purchased through our website) for you and your spouse unless either of you hold one of the following occupations:

  • Motor Trade
  • Retired
  • Housewife/Househusband
  • Student
  • Unemployed
  • Chauffeur working on a hire and reward basis
  • Courier/Driver working on a hire and reward basis and/or delivering goods that they or their employer do not own

In these cases the vehicle may only be used for Social, Domestic and Pleasure (SDP) purposes.

In addition we can't offer insurance where the vehicle is taken Airside (near to aircraft) or is used for paid driving tuition or where the policyholder or their spouse/partner is a foreign diplomat with immunity.

For all other occupations the vehicle may be used in connection with your business or those of your spouse, partner or civil partner (not business partner).

If you require additional drivers to have business use or are uncertain about the type of use you require, please call our Customer Hotline.

If you're an existing customer, your policy documents will confirm which cover applies to the policy.

If you're in doubt about any of the above please contact the Customer Hotline on 0845 246 8701.

Please note: business cover is required where your occupation requires you or your spouse/partner to travel to more than one permanent place of work. You'll need business cover if you or your spouse/partner has more than one occupation.

What's a Named Driver No-Claim Discount?

Named drivers on your Direct Line car insurance policy will earn a Named Driver No Claim Discount in their own name for every continuous claim free policy year, up to a maximum of 5 years. This can be used when they take out their own car insurance policy with Direct Line.

Here's how it works:

'Mark' has insured his son 'Phil' as a named driver on his Direct Line policy for the last three years. Phil buys his own car and wants to take out his own policy with Direct Line. He's been claim free for the last three years on his dad's policy, so is entitled to a discount on his new policy with Direct Line. This is his Named Driver No Claim Discount. At the end of his first year as a policyholder himself he has still not made a claim, so when Phil comes to renew, he'll receive a discount for the three years he was a Named Driver with no claims plus a No Claim Discount for being claim free for one year as a policy holder.

The Named Driver Discount earned will be applied on renewal every year he remains claim free on his Direct Line main policy. If Phil chooses another insurer at this time he'll be able to take his one year earned No Claim Discount with him, however other insurers may not accept the Named Driver No Claim Discount he has earned.

Other points to consider:

  • If Phil's dad Mark leaves Direct Line, Phil has to buy a policy within 90 days to benefit from his earned Named Driver No Claims Discount.
  • If Phil makes a claim whilst he is a Named Driver on Mark's policy, he may lose all or part of his Named Driver No Claim Discount.
  • If Phil, the Named Driver, makes a claim on the policy, his Named Driver No Claim Discount will be reduced by two years for each fault claim.
  • The Named Driver No Claim Discount may be lower than the No Claim Discount of the main policyholder.
  • Named Driver No Claim Discount may not be recognised by other insurers.
  • Named Driver No Claim Discount works differently in that it's applied as a discount to policies held with us and although this discount continues to be added at each renewal along with No Claim Discount, no actual years will be shown on the policy documents.

Taking advantage of this is easy. If you are a Named Driver on a Direct Line car insurance policy and would like to take out your own policy and benefit from this discount, simply call us on 0845 246 0311.

Am I covered to drive in Europe?

Yes, as part of your Direct Line policy you have the minimum level of cover to drive in the EU. This is the equivalent or less than Third Party Only cover, as long as each trip is less than 90 days.  If you'd like to increase your cover to enjoy the same level of cover as you do in the UK e.g. Comprehensive or Third Party, Fire & Theft, purchasing Foreign Use Extension can do this. To purchase this please call us on 0845 246 8706.

How much of my No Claims Discount will I lose if I have an accident?

The policyholder's No Claim Discount (NCD) entitlement will be unaffected unless they exceed 1 fault claim in 1 period of cover, or 2 fault claims in any 3 consecutive periods of cover.

To insure my car, do I need to be the registered keeper?

The policyholder and the registered keeper may be different, but only providing the registered keeper is the policyholder's spouse/partner. Otherwise the policyholder and registered keeper must the same person.
