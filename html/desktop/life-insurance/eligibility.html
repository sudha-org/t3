Are you eligible for one of our life insurance products?

Good to know – life insurance is provided by Legal & General

To take out a life insurance policy with us:

  • You must be aged between 18 and 77 for Level Term Assurance, or 18 and 74 for Mortgage Decreasing Term Assurance.
  • You must be a UK resident.
  • The policy must end by your 80th birthday.
  • If you want to add Critical Illness Cover to your Level Term Assurance policy you must be aged 18 to 67, and the policy must end before your 70th birthday.
  • If you want to add Critical Illness Cover to your Mortgage Decreasing Term Assurance insurance policy you must be aged 18 to 64, and the policy must end before your 70th birthday.
  • Choose a length of cover (the ‘term’) of up to 40 years.
  • You can apply for a policy that covers just you, or you and your partner (called a ‘joint’ policy). With a joint policy it can only pay out the full cash sum once and the cover would then end.
