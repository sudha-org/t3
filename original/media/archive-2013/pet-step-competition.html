Direct Line Pet Insurance 'Pet Step' dance competition

1st November 2013

  • Over 90 dancing pets entered
  • More than 14,000 votes

Over the summer Direct Line ran a nationwide competition to find Britain’s top dancing pets.

With one in five dog owners believing their pets can dance, there was no shortage of entries and social media engagement. More than 90 pets were entered at the dance off website attracting 55,000 visitors and over 14,000 votes.

The following 15 most-voted pets will star in Direct Line’s TV adverts on the Animal Planet channel from this November:

  • Betsey
  • Biffy Phipps
  • Bramble
  • Cezar
  • Dave & Lola
  • Fin
  • Frank
  • Josh
  • Lucky
  • Maximus
  • Nacho
  • Nala
  • Oatsan Barley
  • Samson
  • Sookie

Congratulations to Bramble’s owner, Lisa Forbes, who won a family holiday for four to Hollywood, with the most overall votes.

Whether your furry friend can dance or not, Direct Line is here to help you protect them with a choice of two levels of pet insurance cover.
