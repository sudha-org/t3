Furniture insurance

If you add up the replacement value of the furniture in your home you will see why it’s so vital to invest in adequate home contents insurance cover. Likewise, built-in components such as kitchen units need to be covered under an adequate home buildings policy. Direct Line Home Insurance policies can help provide cover for both.

Direct Line Home Contents Insurance provides cover on a new-for-old basis for household goods, including furniture, carpets, curtains, and your personal belongings in the home, garages and sheds. Contents cover is provided for loss or damage through fire, smoke damage, lightning, theft, flood and escape of water or oil.

Any valuables you have worth over £2,000 will need to be specified on your policy. Direct Line Home Contents Insurance also insures items in your garden up to the value of £1,000.
