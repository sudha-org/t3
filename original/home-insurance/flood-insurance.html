Flood cover

Direct Line flood cover protects your home and contents if they are damaged by burst water pipes or storm flooding.

You'll automatically get this when you take out home insurance with us.

We’ll cover you if…

  • Your home needs rebuilding after water damage
  • You have to live somewhere else for a while
  • Your household items are damaged by flooding

Flood cover from Direct Line

With Direct Line, you'll get...

  • Up to £1,000,000 to cover the cost of rebuilding your home
  • 24x7 access to our service helpline
  • Advice on preparing for and dealing with flooding
