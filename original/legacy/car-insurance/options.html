Our cover levels

Here's everything you need to know about our products at a glance. It gives you a concise summary of our three cover levels, their features and all the optional extras. It even gives you the cover limits.

Comprehensive

This covers you if your car is stolen, damaged by fire or accidentally damaged. You're also covered for claims made against you by other people for bodily injury or damage to their property.

What's as standard

  • Driving
    Other Cars

    You're covered against liability for death or bodily injury to a third party and damage to third party property.

    Comes as standard with all our cover levels.
  • Third Party
    Personal Injury

    If you were involved in a road traffic accident and another person or party was responsible for it, this means you can make a claim for loss or injury.

    Comes as standard with all our cover levels.
  • Multi-car
    Discount

    Do you and your family have more than one car? With our straightforward Multi-car Discount you could save money for every car you insure with us.

    Comes as standard with all our cover levels.
  • Fire
    & Theft

    With Fire and Theft cover you're covered if your car is damaged as a result of theft, attempted theft, fire, lightning or explosion.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive and Third Party, Fire and Theft.
  • In-car
    Equipment

    We cover any permanently fitted in-car audio, TV, telephone or navigation equipment.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive and Third Party, Fire and Theft. Cover limits up to £1,000.
    Read more
  • Medical
    Expenses

    We'll pay medical expenses for each person injured after an accident, as long as there is no cover in force under another car insurance policy.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive.
  • Hotel
    Expenses

    If your car is not driveable after an accident, we'll pay for the cost of an overnight stay in a hotel for you and your passengers. Comes as standard with Comprehensive Cover limits up to £150 for the driver or £250 in total for all the people in the car.

  • Personal
    Belongings

    Your clothing and personal belongings are covered for loss or damage in the event of fire, theft, attempted theft or accident. Keep in mind exclusions apply.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive Cover limits up to £100.
  • Vandalism
    Protection

    We can't protect your car against vandalism. But we can protect your No Claim Discount.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive.
    Read more
  • Damage to a
    third party property

    This covers you for any damage your car causes to another person's car or property.

    Comes as standard with all our cover levels.
  • Repairs guaranteed
    for five years

    Repair work is guaranteed for five years if you use one of our approved repairers and your vehicle will be cleaned inside and out before being returned to you.

    Comes as standard with all our cover levels.
  • Green
    Card

    Once you cross the Channel, you may need an internationally recognised certificate. In short, a Green Card.

    Comes as standard with all our cover levels.
    Read more
  • 24/7 Accident
    Recovery Helpline

    Our accident recovery helpline is available to you at all times.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive.
  • Personal
    Accident

    Designed to protect you in the event of a serious injury sustained during a road accident, this cover can help you with hospital costs or loss of earnings.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive. Cover limits up to £5,000.
    Read more
  • New Car
    Cover

    We will replace your car with a new one of the same make and model if your car is less than 1 year old and you are the first and only registered owner.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive.
  • Uninsured
    Driver Cover

    If an uninsured driver hits you and it's not your fault, we have your excess covered and you will not lose your No Claims Discount. Sounds good, doesn't it?

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive.
  • Replacement
    Child Car Seats

    We take every aspect of your little one's safety very seriously. That’s why in the event of an accident, fire or theft we'll cover the cost of a replacement child car seat - even if there is no apparent damage.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive.
  • Windscreen
    Damage

    If your windscreen or window glass is damaged, we'll pay for their replacement or repair. Give us a call before any work is carried out and we'll direct you to an approved repairer.

    Comes as standard with Comprehensive.

Optional extras

Motor legal protection

Motor Legal Protection helps to recover uninsured losses after a car accident that wasn't your fault. We'll provide up to £100,000 of legal costs to help you to claim, as long as there is a better than 50% chance that your claim will succeed.

Read more

Guaranteed
Hire Car Plus

For a small extra premium, we'll supply a hire car for up to 21 days if your car is unusable or in for repair following a claim (excluding windscreen damage). This applies even if your car is stolen and not recovered or written off as a total loss.

Protected No Claim Discount

After 4 years of driving safely without making a claim or having a claim upheld against you, we give you the chance to protect your hard-earned no claims discount. So if you have an accident, your NCD will remain intact.

Third Party, Fire & Theft

This covers you if other people claim against you for injury or damage to their car or property, and if your car is stolen or damaged by fire.
