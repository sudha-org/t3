Call us to start your claim

Lines are open:

8am - 8pm Monday - Friday, 9am - 5pm Saturday and 11am - 5pm Sunday

Outside these hours you can call our 24 hour accident recovery helpline on 0800 269 015

When you call up you'll be asked for information about:

1


Your personal details

2


What happened and what the damage is to the vehicle

3


Whether the vehicle is driveable

4


Where you are

5


Details of any 3rd parties who were involved in the incident

6


Details of any passengers in you or the 3rd party's car

Even if you don't have the information available, you can still report the claim.
