Multi Car Discounts

With Direct Line’s multi-car discount, we help you save money when you and your family have more than one car in the household.

  • You’ll get a discount for every car you insure with us
  • Your No Claim Discount will not be affected by other drivers on a policy
  • Every car you insure can keep its own renewal date
  • Direct Line multi-car discount is available to new and existing customers
