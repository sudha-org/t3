What's as standard

  • Buildings insurance up to £1m

    With our buildings insurance you can cover the main structure of your home. You'll get cover up to £1,000,000 if you need to rebuild or repair your home after:

    • Fire, lightning, or explosion
    • Storm or flood damage - excluding fences and gates
    • Theft and vandalism
    • Subsidence or heave
    • Escape of water
    • It also includes:
    • Garages and outbuildings
    • Permanent fixtures like kitchens, bathrooms and built-in wardrobes
    • Up to £25,000 cover for alternative accommodation for you and your family if necessary after an insured event
    • Access to our 24-hour emergency helpline


  • Contents insurance up to £50,000

    Our contents insurance covers the value of all your possessions in your home. This means you get:

    • Up to £50,000 cover as standard
    • Up to £500 cover for cash in your home
    • Up to £1000 cover for garden items.
    • Cover against fire, smoke damage and lightning, theft, flood and leaking water or oil.
    • Unlimited cover for fridge and freezer contents.
    • Replacement locks and keys if your keys are lost or stolen.
    • Up to £5,000 worth of cover for your children's contents when they're living away from home
      • at university*. No need to register for it – it's automatically included.
    • An automatic 10% increase in contents cover for several weeks either side of religious
      • festivals, family weddings and the birth of your child.
    • Access to our 24-hour emergency helpline.
    • Cover on a 'new-for-old' basis for:
    • Household goods, such as furniture, carpets, curtains
    • Belongings in the home, garages and sheds - including clothing
    • This applies to student possessions kept in lodgings at university.


  • 24-hour UK emergency helpline
    • Our home emergency helpline is here when you need us.
