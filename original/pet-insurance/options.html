Our pet insurance
cover options

Choose from two levels of vet fee cover – Essential and Advanced. Both come with some optional additional benefits that you can choose from in order to suit your individual needs.

Advanced Cover

A more comprehensive level of cover for greater peace of mind.

  • Vets' fees up to £8,000 per condition (double the limit of our Essential Cover)
  • No time limits on any condition
  • Cover for an unlimited number of short and long-term conditions
  • Legal advice line
  • Bereavement helpline
  • Find-a-Vet helpline

What's included


  • Vets' fees

    Get extra peace of mind with double cover limits in vets’ fees per condition when compared to our Essential Cover, and no time limits (provided your cover is still in force). This includes:


    • Up to £45 towards consultation fees for each visit to the vet
    • Up to £110 towards additional fees for each out-of-hours visit
    • Up to £70 towards histology fees (the microscopic study of tissue)
    • Up to £70 towards cytology fees (the microscopic study of cells)
    • Up to £35 per session towards hydrotherapy and physiotherapy fees

  • No time limits on any condition

    There are no time limits on any condition – e.g. arthritis - if you choose our Advanced Cover.

  • Cover for an unlimited number of short and long-term conditions

    This means you’re covered if your pet develops a short or a long-term condition such as diabetes.

  • Legal advice line

    Owning a pet comes with many responsibilities. Your Advanced Cover gives you access to practical legal advice on any personal problem, whether or not it relates to an incident or issue involving your pet. Our legal team is here to explain complicated legal issues in plain language and in a helpful and friendly way. Give them a call at 0870 600 0633.

  • Bereavement helpline

    It can sometimes be difficult to come to terms with the death of a much-loved pet. Call our professional team on 0870 600 0633 and share your thoughts and feelings with them. Get advice and support on how to cope with grief.

  • Find-a-Vet helpline

    If for some reason you can’t contact your usual vet, or you and your pet are away from home in the UK and need a vet, then you can call 0870 600 0633 for help. Our advisors can provide you with details of a vet’s practice near you.

All our pet advice lines are open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. UK-based call centres.

Optional extras


5 extras in 1
package

These additional benefits come as a single package.

  • Accidental death
    We know it’s difficult but if your pet is fatally hurt in an accident, we’ll support you by paying the purchase price up to £1,500.
  • Advertising and reward
    Get up to £1,000 towards advertising if your pet goes missing. This includes the cost of getting your pet home and a reward of up to £200.
  • Lost pet
    If your pet is stolen or strays and isn’t found within 45 days, then we’ll support you by paying the purchase price up to £1,500.
  • Pet boarding fees when you’re in hospital
    Up to £1,000 towards the cost of having your pet looked after if you have to stay in hospital.
  • Holiday cancellation
    Up to £5,000 to cover expenses you can’t get back from anywhere else if you have to cancel or cut short your holiday because your pet goes missing or needs unexpected life saving treatment just before or during your trip.



Third party liability
(dogs only)

Get up to £2 million cover if your dog injures someone or causes damage to property.



Overseas
travel

Extend your policy to cover journeys to countries included in the UK Government’s Pet Travel Scheme. This includes:

  • Quarantine costs – up to £1,500
  • Loss of your pet’s passport – up to £250
  • Repeat tapeworm treatment
  • Emergency expenses abroad – up to £300




What's not included

  • Claims for any illness or disease arising during the first 14 days of the pet being covered.
  • Claims for or connected with, or a recurrence of, a pre-existing condition, symptom or event.
  • Claims for behavioural problems or traits.
  • The use of your pet for commercial, guard, breeding, security or working purposes, or for racing, or any claims arising from these uses.
  • Claims as a result of your pet worrying livestock of any kind, including horses whether kept for work or leisure purposes.
  • Claims arising from or related to, or costs associated with criminal proceedings or fines.
  • Any payment for any claim that results from an incident covered by other insurance unless the cover provided by that insurance has been used up.
