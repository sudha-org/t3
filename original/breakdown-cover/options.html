Our Breakdown cover options

We've got five levels of breakdown cover. Choose the one that's right for you.

Rescue

Get roadside assistance once you're ¼ mile from home and, if your car can't be fixed at the roadside, we'll take you and your car to a nearby garage. We also offer a message service to let friends and family know what has happened.

  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • No call out charge

What's optional

Personal cover

With all our options, your car is covered whoever is driving it. For an additional premium, you can choose Personal Cover which covers you whatever car you're travelling in – even as a passenger. We're also giving away two for one on personal cover - so you can add your spouse or partner at no extra cost.

How do you get Personal Cover? It's simple - you can obtain a quote online. Just click on the personal cover option once on the quotation screen and then hit recalculate.

Personal Cover is only available for cars under 16 years old that are both privately registered and being used in the UK.



Family cover

Our Family Cover covers up to 3 additional members of your household you tell us about when driving or travelling in any car in the UK.

How do you get Family Cover?

Just give us a call at 0845 246 2766 to get a quote.

Family Cover is only available for cars under 16 years old that are both privately registered and being used in the UK.
