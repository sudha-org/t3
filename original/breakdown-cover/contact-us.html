Typetalk

Direct Line supports the Typetalk service for people with communication difficulties. To use Typetalk, you’ll need to prefix the number 18001 before the number you wish to dial. For example: 18001 + area code + number.

Calls may be monitored and recorded. Call charges will vary.

How much does Direct Line Breakdown Cover cost?

Our prices match your circumstances (e.g. car, where you live, and age) like car insurance. If you have a reliable car that you maintain well, you pay less than someone with an old, unreliable car. Our prices are tailored to the person and their car, just like car insurance.

Do I have breakdown cover if I'm travelling in someone else's car?

We give you the choice to decide if you need this level of cover with our Personal Cover option.

Who should I call if my car breaks down?

Please call us on 0800 590 590.

Do I have breakdown cover if someone else drives my car?

With all our cover levels, your car is covered whoever is driving it.
