Bodily Injury Direct

If you’ve had a car accident where a Direct Line policyholder was fully liable, we’d like to help.

Bodily Injury Direct provides an efficient, completely free service offering you full compensation. It means you don’t have to pay for injury solicitors or involve claims management companies.

Instead, one of our Bodily Injury Consultants will deal with your case from beginning to end. If you need treatment, we’ll arrange for a rehabilitation specialist to agree a treatment plan with you.

Why does Bodily Injury Direct make sense?

  • We handle your claim quickly, professionally and free of charge
  • We offer a complete service, including car repairs, a rental car if required, rehabilitation and compensation
  • There’s no contract or other paperwork
  • Any compensation will be full and free of deductions
  • All your agreed medical treatment is free
