Example – How this works

To give you an indication of how much a monthly premium could cost, we’ve put together some examples*

  • If you’re a 25 year old non-smoker, £100,000 cover over 35 years would cost you just £6.84 per month
  • If you’re a 35 year old non-smoker, £100,000 cover over 25 years would cost you just £7.72 per month
  • If you’re a 35 year old non-smoker, £150,000 cover over 25 years would cost you just £10.82 per month
  • If you’re a 45 year old non-smoker, £100,000 cover over 15 years would cost you just £12.68 per month

*Prices as at 23rd May 2014 for existing Direct Line customers taking a Level Term Assurance policy. Remember the price may change based on the answers in the application.
