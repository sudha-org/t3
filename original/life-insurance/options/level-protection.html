Level Term Assurance?

Good to know – life insurance is provided by Legal & General

Level Term Assurance cover means your premiums and cash sum payout stay the same, unless you make changes to your policy. Designed to help protect your family financially, life insurance can pay out a cash sum if you die during the policy term – your loved ones can use it however they wish.

Did you know that Level Term Assurance can also help cover an "interest only" type mortgage?

Main features at a glance:

  • Premiums start at £6 per month, and stay fixed throughout your policy, unless you make changes to your policy. This lets you budget with confidence.
  • Level Term Assurance cover means your cash sum payout stays the same.
  • You choose how much you want to be covered for. If you’re unsure take a look at the Life insurance calculator, designed to help you decide.
  • Terminal Illness Cover could pay out your chosen amount of cover if you're diagnosed with a terminal illness and have a life expectancy of 12 months or less. This cover is not available during the last 12 months of your policy or for terms of less than 2 years. Terms and conditions apply, please read the policy Terms and Conditions for more information.
  • You can add Critical Illness Cover for an extra cost when you take out a life policy, giving you extra protection. This covers 41 conditions.
  • Guaranteed Insurability Option lets you increase your cover as your circumstances change, for example if you got married, entered into a registered civil partnership, or you move house. Terms and conditions apply, please read the Terms and Conditions for more information.

Things you need to know:

  • If you intentionally take your own life in the first 12 months of your cover, your policy will be cancelled and will not pay out. However, all premiums paid will be refunded.
  • If you stop paying premiums before the end of your policy, your cover will end 30 days after your missed payment.
  • This is not a savings or investment product and has no cash value unless a valid claim is made.
  • Please read the policy Terms and Conditions before you apply.
  • It is your responsibility to make sure the policy meets your needs. If you need advice please contact a financial adviser. Keep in mind you may be charged for any advice provided.
